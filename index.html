How can I use green.dj?
It is simple. Just click 'Stable builds' tab, find newest version (probably the first one) and drag the button to your bookmark bar. When at plug.dj, simply click that bookmark.
I don't have my bookmark bar.
Try using Ctrl+Shift+B. If it doesn't help, try searching through browser settings.
